Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make a monthly budget?

Start by listing your monthly income, followed by fixed and variable expenses. Subtract total expenses from income to find your surplus or deficit.

What is the 50/30/20 budgeting rule?

It’s a method where 50% of your income goes to needs, 30% to wants, and 20% to savings or debt repayment.

Is this tool suitable for couples or families?

Yes, you can add multiple income sources and expenses to reflect your household’s budget.

Can I use this for student budgeting?

Absolutely. It’s ideal for tracking tuition, rent, groceries, and other expenses with limited income sources like part-time jobs or scholarships.
