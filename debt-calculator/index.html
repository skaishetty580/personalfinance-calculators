Frequently Asked Questions

How does the debt snowball method work?

With the snowball method, you pay off your smallest debts first while making minimum payments on larger debts. This builds momentum and motivation.

What is the debt avalanche method?

The avalanche method focuses on paying off debts with the highest interest rates first, helping you save the most on interest over time.

Can I add extra payments?

Yes. Our calculator allows you to enter extra monthly payments to see how much faster you can be debt-free and how much interest you’ll save.

Is this calculator good for credit card debt?

Absolutely. You can enter multiple credit card balances and interest rates to see your custom repayment plan.
