Frequently Asked Questions

How much do I need to retire?

It depends on your desired retirement lifestyle, expected expenses, and retirement age. A common rule is to save 25x your annual expenses.

How does this calculator work?

You input your income, savings, retirement age, expected expenses, and it projects your future savings, adjusted for inflation and growth rate.

Does it include Social Security or pension?

You can optionally include estimated monthly Social Security or pension income in the retirement income section.

What is a safe withdrawal rate?

A common guideline is the 4% rule, which suggests withdrawing 4% of your savings per year in retirement to make your money last.
